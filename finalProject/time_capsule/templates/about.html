About Us

The goal of this webapp is to allow users to store their memories. Through the time capsule features made available on this site, one can create snapshots of their current lives, feelings, and personality. Once a time capsule is saved, the user can choose a time it will unlock. Unitl then, the contents of the time capsule will be unavailable. The goal is that when opening a time capsule, the user can get a glimpse into their own past. This program is meant to foster both current reflection and future recollection.

Note: this site is created by a college student (me) as a part of a course project.

Features

This app currently has two time capsule features:

1. Letter

2. Form

The letter option is marketed as a letter to your future self. This feature gives the user the freedom to write about whatever they want.

The form option is a more guided time capsule creation experience. It is designed to allow users to answer different questions made for them to record their current life and mindset.
